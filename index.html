Muhammad Turab

Affiliations. Dublin City University, Dublin 09, Ireland..

I am a doctoral researcher in the Insight SFI Research Centre for Data Analytics at the School of Computing Dublin City University (DCU), Ireland. My research interests include computer vision, medical imaging, explainable AI (XAI), knolwedge extraction, knowledge representation, graph analysis, LLMs, and Agentic AI.

I am also an Erasmus Mundus Joint masters degree holder in Computational Color and Spectral Imaging (COSI) at Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU), University of Jean Monnet (UJM), France and University of Eastern Finland (UEF).
